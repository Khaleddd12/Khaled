    • Football, also known as association football or soccer, is the world's most popular sport. It is played by millions of people in more than 200 countries. Two teams of 11 players each compete to get the ball into the other team's goal. The team with the higher number of goals at the end of the match wins.

    Football Field

    A football match is played on a rectangular field with two goals, one on each end of the field. The field length ranges from 100 to 130 yards and width from 50 to 100 yards. The goals are composed of two vertical posts that are joined by a horizontal crossbar.

    Common Football Rules

    • No hands allowed - only the goalkeeper can touch the ball with hands, other players can kick, head, chest, etc. the ball to pass, carry, or score
    • Offside - attacking player cannot be ahead of the ball and have fewer than two defending players between them and the goal line
    • No dangerous play - no fouling, pushing, tripping or dangerous tackles that can cause injury
    • Free kicks - opponents must stand 10 yards from the ball for corners, penalties, kick-offs, direct and indirect free kicks
    • Throw-ins - when the ball goes out of play, it is thrown in by the opposite team of the player who last touched it
